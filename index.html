Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024

1. Information We Collect

Wedding Planner Hub is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Camera Access: We request camera permission solely for the purpose of taking photos within the app, such as capturing wedding inspiration, venue photos, or scanning documents related to wedding planning.
  • App Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data to improve app performance and user experience.
  • Personal Information: Any wedding planning data you enter (guest lists, budgets, schedules) is stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide core app functionality (camera features for photo capture)
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To troubleshoot technical issues
  • To personalize your wedding planning experience

3. Camera Usage

The camera permission is used exclusively for:

  • Taking photos of wedding venues, decorations, and inspiration
  • Capturing images for your wedding mood boards
  • Scanning documents related to wedding planning

Important: All photos taken with the camera are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your photos to our servers or any third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your wedding planning data is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, but remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

Wedding Planner Hub does not share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to protect our rights.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and export your data from the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Withdraw camera permission through your device settings

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
